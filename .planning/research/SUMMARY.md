# Research Summary: v1.2 Notes, Browser & Platform Growth

**Stack additions:** no framework change needed; extend local Notes storage/search helpers, Browser navigation policy helpers, and repo-driven app manifest/catalog metadata.

**Feature table stakes:** searchable/organized Notes, direct Browser destination entry with truthful fallback, contributor app submission metadata, and a first in-product app catalog.

**Watch Out For:** sync/editor scope creep in Notes, dishonest “full browser” claims, and turning submission/catalog groundwork into a full marketplace too early.

## Recommendation

Keep `v1.2` focused on three things:

1. make Notes more useful locally
2. make Browser more capable without lying
3. make the internal app platform visible to contributors and users through submission/catalog foundations

This supports a clean five-phase milestone: Notes expansion, Browser expansion, submission foundations, catalog surface, and integrated verification.
